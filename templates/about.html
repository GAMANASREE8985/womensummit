About Us

Organizing women summit is crucial for several reasons. Firstly, such events provide a platform for women and girls to come together, share their experiences, and build a sense of solidarity and community. They offer opportunities for networking, mentorship, and skill-building, empowering participants to pursue their goals and aspirations. Secondly, women summit raise awareness about issues affecting women and girls, including gender inequality, discrimination, and violence.

Through workshops, discussions, and performances, these events educate attendees and inspire them to take action towards positive change. Additionally, women summit celebrate the achievements and talents of women and girls, promoting their visibility and recognition in society. By organizing these summits, we create inclusive spaces where women and girls feel valued, supported, and empowered to realize their full potential


The Women Development Cell (WDC) at KLEF promotes gender equality through education, advocacy, and skill-building initiatives. Our mission includes creating a safe environment for women to thrive, offering awareness programs on women's rights, and conducting gender sensitization activities on campus. We strive to address gender discrimination, prevent sexual harassment, and ensure equitable opportunities for all genders within the university community.
